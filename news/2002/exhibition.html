International Congress of Mathematical Software   

Call for Exhibitions


 
The first International Congress of Mathematical Software (ICM2002) will be held in Beijing, China, from August 17 to 19, 2002. The program of ICMS2002 consists of six plenary lectures and nine invited lectures by leading experts in the field, 42 contributedpresentations, and 20 poster presentations. We estimate that there will be more than one hundred participants. For more information about ICMS, please visit the web site http://www.mathsoftware.org.

ICMS2002 is a satellite conference of International Congress of Mathematics 2002 (ICM2002), which will be held in Beijing from August 20 to 26, 2002. ICM is the premier conference of the mathematics community. ICM2002 has near four thousands registered participants.

Upon the request of some participants, we decided to set up an exhibition room in the conference site of ICMS2002.  Materials from publishers, software companies, mathematical societies are welcome to have their products exhibited.  A minimal fee will be charged for each exhibition position: $400 for commercial companies and $100 for academic software. If you are interested in this, please contact the following person before July 20th, 2002.
