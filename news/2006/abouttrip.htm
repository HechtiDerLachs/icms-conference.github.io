Second International Conference on Mathematical Software
ICMS'2006

Castro Urdiales, SPAIN, September 1-3 2006



 

Social Events

 

 

Conference Banquet

The banquet will take place in the restaurant Hotel Las Rocas  on  September 2 at 20:45.

The ICMS 2006 registration  includes one banquet ticket. Additional banquet tickets for accompanying people  can be  bought at the registration desk. A ticket costs 50 EUR, a child  ticket costs 30 EUR.

Menu

  • Salad of avocado and prawns.
  • Tuna  to the Calabrian.
  • Sirloin of veal with garrison of vegetables.
  • Band of puff-pastry with cream and fruits
     
  • Red wine Rioja Viña Izadi
  • White wine Rueda Viña Moncen
  • Mineral water
  • Coffee/tea
     

Requests for vegetarian menu can be declared  on the  registration desk.

 

Post-ICMS 2006 Excursion (September 4, 2006)

The ICMS2006   Local Organizers are planning to offer for ICMS attendees and accompanying persons the possibility to do some sightseeing in  Santander. 

A tentative schedule and program

  • 10:00  Meeting at the hall of  the conference venue La Residencia
     
  • 10:45 Travelling  by bus to Santander.
     
  • 11:45 Walking to  Magdalena Palace
     
  • 12:45 Visit  Sardinero   beaches
     
  • 14:30 Lunch in Riojano Restaurant
     
  • 16:30 Visit Menendez Pelayo Library.
     
  • 18:00  Visit the Cathedral  of Santander and other historical buildings.
     
  • 20:15   Return to Castro Urdiales.
     

Registration Procedure and fee

  • Participants who are interested in the excursion  will have to communicate it in the registration desk during the congress
     
  •  Registration fee doesn't include this social event and, it will be at the expenses of each one.
     
  •  More information about this issue will be posted in the registration desk.
