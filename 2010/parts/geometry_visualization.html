Session title

Geometry and Visualization

Organizers

Tim Hoffmann (TU Muenchen)
Wayne Rossman (Kobe University)

Keywords

mathematical visualization, geometry, applications in computer graphics and geometry processing, discretization of geometry

Description

This session will focus on software tools and techniques for mathematical visualization, geometry processing, the discretization of surfaces for visualization, simulation, and computations, and related topics.
