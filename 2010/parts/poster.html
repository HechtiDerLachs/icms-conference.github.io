Session title

Poster Session

Organizer

Raimundas Vidunas (Kobe University)

Description

Submissions for the poster session on any subject of the conference are welcome until August 30, 2010 at the latest. Earlier submissions are appreciated. Willing participants should submit a single page PDF file (generated by an A0 poster macro like a0poster.cls) by email to Raimundas Vidunas ( icms2010-poster@math.kobe-u.ac.jp ). The posters will be printed on A0 sheets by the organizers.
