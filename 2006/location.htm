Second International Conference on Mathematical Software
ICMS'2006

Castro Urdiales, SPAIN, September 1-3 2006


Location

The workshop will be held in Castro Urdiales, one of the most beautiful places of the north coast of Spain. Castro Urdiales is an old fishermen's town with magnificient buildings, beautiful promenades, a very rich history and a lovely combination between the antique and the modern.

More information about the history of Castro Urdiales (in Spanish, English and French) can be found here.

The most remarkable monuments are described (in Spanish, English and French) here. All of them are at a walking distance from the conference venue.

Conference venue

The conference venue will be La Residencia, a very recently rebuilt palace. The building has the most advanced audio-visual equipment for conferences and presentations and offers a unique indoors-outdoors environment for both fruitful discussions and relaxing enjoyment.
