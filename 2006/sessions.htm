Second International Conference on Mathematical Software
ICMS'2006

Castro Urdiales, SPAIN, September 1-3 2006



List of Sessions

This conference is comprised of some sessions devoted to different Mathematical Software issues as well as a general track. As a rule, the general track will consists of all accepted papers/presentations which do not belong to any of the sessions listed below.
