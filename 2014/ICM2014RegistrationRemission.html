A partial ICM registration fee remission
Note: If you are attending ICM as well, then ICM will reimburse 30% of your ICMS registration fee. Only full-paying registrations at ICMS is eligible. E.g., You can be reimbursed KRW 96,000 if you pay early registration fee of KRW 320,000. The reimbursement will be made in cash on ICM conference site.

Important: Please, keep the receipt of ICMS2014 to receive the reimbursement (refer to this page).
