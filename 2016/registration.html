To register for ICMS 2016, please follow this registration link.

Participants who have also registered for the 7ECM are granted a 20% discount on registration fees.

The registration fee covers the proceedings, congress materials, welcome reception, two coffee breaks per day, social dinner (except for a small contribution) and a city tour.

 

ICMS2016 - Social Dinner

The price for the Social Dinner is regular 20,- € and for students 10,- €. Accompanying persons have to pay 80,- €. The dinner takes place at the TV tower (Fernsehturm am Alexanderplatz). Please note that this place is not disable-adapted. Accompanying persons for the excursion (city tour) have to pay 15,- €.

 

ICMS2016 – Excursion

The excursion will take place directly after the conference and will consist of a city tour in doubledecker cabriolet busses. The tour will start at ZIB and will include the most important touristic highlights of Berlin: Kurfürstendamm, Kranzler Eck, Gedächtniskirche, KaDeWe, Kulturforum und Philharmonie, Siegessäule, Tiergarten, Schloss Bellevue, Leipziger Platz, Potsdamer Platz, Government District, Reichstag, Bundeskanzleramt, Hauptbahnhof, Pariser Platz, Brandenburg Gate, Stelenfeld, Unter den Linden, Bebelplatz, Schlossplatz, Museumsinsel, Friedrichstraße (Checkpoint Charlie), Gendarmenmarkt, Bundesrat, Ministries and much more.

There will be English-speaking guides explaining everything to you and answering your questions. You will receive information on the urban development in the last few years as well as on Berlin as capital, as city of modern architecture as well as city full of German history.

It will take approximately two hours and end in the center of Berlin.
