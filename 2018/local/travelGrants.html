Travel Grants
A limited number of travel grants, which include free conference registration, are expected for graduate students, postdocs, early career faculty, members of under-represented groups, and researchers without federal support.

Members of Participating Organizations of the Institute for Mathematics and Its Aplpications (IMA) can use their IMA travel funds to attend. Contact your respresentative for more details.

Deadline: May 11, 2018

Instructions:
  • Email the following to acms@nd.edu with subject ICMS Travel Grant:
    • Name
    • Affiliation and its location
    • Eligibility criterion (select all that apply):
      • graduate student
      • postdoc
      • early career faculty
      • member of under-represented groups
      • researcher without federal support
    • Name of advisor/mentor and their affiliation (for graduate students and postdocs only)
    • A current curriculum vitae (CV)
    • Budget request form listing amount of funding requested (no justification/reciepts are needed at this time)

  • For graduate students and postdocs:
    • Your advisor/mentor must send a brief letter of recommendation to acms@nd.edu with subject Recommendation for ICMS Travel Grant
